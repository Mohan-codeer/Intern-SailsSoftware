# Week 3
## LoRA
- It helps us teach AI new things quickly.
- We do not change the whole AI, only a small part of it.
- This saves a lot of computer power and time.

## GRPO
- This is a way to help AI learn from its own mistakes.
- The AI looks at many answers and picks the best one.
- It does this without needing a second AI to check its work.

## Thinking Models (Powerful AI)
- These models stop and `think` before they give an answer.
- They check their own logic to make sure it is correct.
- This helps them solve very hard math and science problems.

## RLHF
- People read what the AI writes and give it a score.
- The AI learns to talk in a way that humans like and trust.
- It helps the AI stay helpful and follow the rules.

## SFT
- We give the AI many good examples of how to answer.
- The AI copies these examples to learn the right format.
- This is usually the first step in teaching an AI how to behave.
